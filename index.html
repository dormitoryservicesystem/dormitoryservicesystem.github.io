What is this all about ?

We will design an application that solves some problems for dormitory services.This application will contain a membership system,a payment system and a gps system.With this app studends will be able to book a service until half an hour before the service hour.Thus,drivers will see how many students will use the service at a time.

The Main Problems

-How many students will use the service during the service hour.

-Service charges are not paid on time.

Previous Solutions to These Problems

-Service Bus Departure Time Schedules

-Warning people who didnt pay the charge

Our Solutions

-A mobile app for students to reserve a place for the next bus

-GPS system in each services for students to see the current location from mobile app

-Dormitory cards with chip

-Card reading system in each services

-A membership system for students and drivers

-Online payment system for students to pay charge easly

Who will use this system ?

-This system is for students who stay at the dormitory and use the service busses
